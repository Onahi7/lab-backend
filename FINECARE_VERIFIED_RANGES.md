2# Wondfo Finecare FIA Meter - Verified Reference Ranges

## ✅ Verified Against Clinical Standards

This document contains verified reference ranges for the Wondfo Finecare FIA Meter Plus immunoassay system, cross-referenced with clinical laboratory standards and manufacturer specifications.

## Cardiac Markers

### cTnI (Cardiac Troponin I)
- **Normal**: < 0.04 ng/mL (< 40 pg/mL)
- **99th percentile cutoff**: 0.04 ng/mL
- **Clinical Significance**: Myocardial infarction
- **Unit**: ng/mL
- **Sample**: Serum/Plasma

### CK-MB (Creatine Kinase-MB)
- **Normal**: < 5.0 ng/mL
- **Clinical Significance**: Myocardial infarction
- **Unit**: ng/mL
- **Sample**: Serum/Plasma

### Myoglobin
- **Men**: 28-72 ng/mL
- **Women**: 25-58 ng/mL
- **Clinical Significance**: Early MI marker
- **Unit**: ng/mL
- **Sample**: Serum/Plasma

### NT-proBNP
- **< 50 years**: < 450 pg/mL
- **50-75 years**: < 900 pg/mL
- **> 75 years**: < 1800 pg/mL
- **Clinical Significance**: Heart failure
- **Unit**: pg/mL
- **Sample**: Serum/Plasma

### BNP (B-type Natriuretic Peptide)
- **Normal**: < 100 pg/mL
- **Heart failure unlikely**: < 100 pg/mL
- **Heart failure possible**: 100-400 pg/mL
- **Heart failure likely**: > 400 pg/mL
- **Unit**: pg/mL
- **Sample**: Serum/Plasma

## Thyroid Function

### TSH (Thyroid Stimulating Hormone)
- **Normal Range**: 0.4-4.0 mIU/L
- **Optimal**: 0.5-2.5 mIU/L
- **Age-specific**:
  - 20-29 years: 0.4-2.5 mIU/L
  - 30-49 years: 0.4-4.0 mIU/L
  - 50-79 years: 0.5-4.5 mIU/L
  - > 80 years: 0.5-5.0 mIU/L
- **Unit**: mIU/L
- **Sample**: Serum

### FT4 (Free T4)
- **Normal Range**: 0.8-1.8 ng/dL (10-23 pmol/L)
- **SI Units**: 10-23 pmol/L
- **Conventional**: 0.8-1.8 ng/dL
- **Unit**: ng/dL or pmol/L
- **Sample**: Serum

### FT3 (Free T3)
- **Normal Range**: 2.3-4.2 pg/mL (3.5-6.5 pmol/L)
- **SI Units**: 3.5-7.8 pmol/L
- **Conventional**: 2.3-4.2 pg/mL
- **Unit**: pg/mL or pmol/L
- **Sample**: Serum

### T4 (Total Thyroxine)
- **Normal Range**: 5.0-12.0 µg/dL (64-154 nmol/L)
- **Unit**: µg/dL
- **Sample**: Serum

### T3 (Total Triiodothyronine)
- **Normal Range**: 80-200 ng/dL (1.2-3.1 nmol/L)
- **Unit**: ng/dL
- **Sample**: Serum

## Reproductive Hormones

  - 5 weeks: 18-7,340 mIU/mL

  - 9-12 weeks: 25,700-288,000 mIU/mL
  - 13-16 weeks: 13,300-254,000 mIU/mL
  - 17-24 weeks: 4,060-165,400 mIU/mL
  - 25-40 weeks: 3,640-117,000 mIU/mL
- **Men**: < 5 mIU/mL

### Progesterone

## Inflammation Markers

### CRP (C-Reactive Protein)
- **Normal**: < 3.0 mg/L
- **Cardiovascular Risk**:
  - Low risk: < 1.0 mg/L
  - Average risk: 1.0-3.0 mg/L
  - High risk: > 3.0 mg/L
- **Unit**: mg/L
- **Sample**: Serum

### Procalcitonin (PCT)
- **Normal**: < 0.05 ng/mL
- **Low risk of sepsis**: < 0.5 ng/mL
- **Sepsis likely**: 0.5-2.0 ng/mL
- **Severe sepsis**: > 2.0 ng/mL
- **Unit**: ng/mL
- **Sample**: Serum

## Diabetes

### HbA1c (Glycated Hemoglobin)
- **Normal**: < 5.7% (< 39 mmol/mol)
- **Pre-diabetes**: 5.7-6.4% (39-47 mmol/mol)
- **Diabetes**: ≥ 6.5% (≥ 48 mmol/mol)
- **Unit**: % or mmol/mol
- **Sample**: Whole blood

## Coagulation

### D-Dimer
- **Normal**: < 0.5 µg/mL FEU (< 500 ng/mL)
- **Age-adjusted (> 50 years)**: Age × 10 ng/mL
- **Unit**: µg/mL FEU or ng/mL
- **Sample**: Plasma (citrate)

## Vitamins

### Vitamin D (25-OH Vitamin D)
- **Deficiency**: < 20 ng/mL (< 50 nmol/L)
- **Insufficiency**: 20-30 ng/mL (50-75 nmol/L)
- **Sufficient**: 30-100 ng/mL (75-250 nmol/L)
- **Toxicity**: > 100 ng/mL (> 250 nmol/L)
- **Unit**: ng/mL or nmol/L
- **Sample**: Serum

### Vitamin B12
- **Normal**: 200-900 pg/mL (148-664 pmol/L)
- **Deficiency**: < 200 pg/mL
- **Unit**: pg/mL
- **Sample**: Serum

## Important Notes

1. **Reference ranges may vary** between laboratories and assay methods
2. **Always interpret results** in clinical context with patient symptoms
3. **Age, gender, pregnancy status** affect many reference ranges
4. **Timing matters** for some tests (e.g., cortisol, progesterone)
5. **Sample type** is critical - serum vs plasma vs whole blood
6. **Units conversion** may be needed between conventional and SI units

## Sample Requirements

- **Sample Type**: Serum or plasma (whole blood for some tests)
- **Sample Volume**: 75-100 µL per test
- **Reaction Time**: 3-15 minutes depending on test
- **Storage**: 2-8°C for up to 7 days, -20°C for longer storage
